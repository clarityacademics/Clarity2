Programs & Services

Three Tiers.
One Philosophy.

Every program is built around the same belief: the right support should make a student calmer, more capable, and more confident — not just better at test prep.

Flagship Program

Clarity Elite

"From overwhelmed and reactive to clear, proactive, and grounded."

Best for: whole-student support Book a Consult

When school starts touching everything — your child's sleep, mood, confidence, and even how they talk about God — Clarity Elite steps in as a steady guide. We combine high-level tutoring with coaching on mindset, organization, and faith-shaped resilience.

Over time, grades stabilize, evenings calm down, and your child starts to see themselves not as "behind," but as a capable learner with a future they can actually picture.

  • Deep subject tutoring in Math and Science (including AP-level courses)
  • Executive function coaching: organization, planning, and follow-through
  • Mindset and resilience work woven into every session
  • Regular parent partnership check-ins and progress updates
  • Between-session access and support for urgent questions
  • Whole-student care: sleep, stress, habits, and confidence addressed alongside content

1-on-1 Tutoring

Clarity Core

"If I show up and do the plan, I can handle this."

Best for: students slipping through the cracks Book a Consult

Clarity Core is for the student who can learn the material but keeps slipping through the cracks — missed points, rushed homework, shaky test prep. In a consistent 1:1 space, we slow things down, clear up confusion, and build solid study habits right alongside content.

The result is less panicked cramming, more predictable performance, and a student who knows exactly what to do when they sit down to study.

  • Weekly 1:1 sessions focused on the student's current coursework
  • Clear explanations of concepts — not just solving the homework
  • Study habit and test prep strategies built into every session
  • Steady, low-pressure accountability week over week
  • Parent updates on progress and areas of focus

Small Group

Clarity Cohort

"Learning lighter, more normal, and a lot less scary."

Best for: students who learn alongside others Book a Consult

Some students open up the moment they realize they're not the only one who doesn't "get it" yet. Clarity Cohort turns that moment into a weekly rhythm — small, carefully led groups where students ask real questions, work through hard problems, and see how others think.

Over time, they stop feeling isolated or "behind," and start feeling part of a community that makes school feel less intimidating and a lot more manageable.

  • Small group sessions (3–6 students) with guided instruction
  • Collaborative problem-solving and peer learning built into every class
  • Structured curriculum aligned to students' current coursework
  • A safe environment to ask questions without fear of judgment
  • More affordable than 1:1 while still offering personal attention

Frequently Asked Questions

A few of the most common questions families ask before getting started.

Can we start with Core or Cohort and move up to Elite later?+

Yes. Many families start with Core or Cohort to stabilize grades, then move into Elite when they're ready for deeper coaching and between-session access. We'll help you decide if and when that shift makes sense.

Does my student have to be in AP Physics to join Elite?+

No. AP Physics Elite is one path, but the Hybrid Coaching + Tutoring path is open to students in upper-level math or science who need whole-picture support. We'll talk through courses, goals, and bandwidth in your consult.

Do you work with students who are already doing "fine" in school?+

Absolutely. Some Elite students already have strong grades but feel constantly behind, anxious, or unsure how to sustain their pace. Elite can help them shift from grinding to grounded.

How does scheduling and billing work?+

We'll agree on a weekly time (or times) that works with your family's rhythms. Billing is handled in simple, predictable blocks — no surprise add-ons. Full policy details are shared before you commit, and we'll walk through them together.

What if my student is anxious or hesitant about coaching?+

That's normal. We never force vulnerability. The first sessions focus on calm, concrete wins: understanding the material better, getting organized, reducing "surprise" stress. Trust tends to follow results.

What happens if we need to pause or change tiers?+

Life happens. If you need to pause or switch tiers, we'll talk through options and keep communication clear so your student never feels blindsided by adult logistics.
